1. Who is known as the "Queen of Disco"?




Answer Info: Donna Summer is considered the Queen of Disco due to her iconic tracks like "Last Dance" and "Hot Stuff."

2. Which dance style became popular with disco music?




Answer Info: The Hustle became a popular partner dance in the 1970s, widely performed at disco clubs.

3. What movie helped popularize disco music globally?




Answer Info: "Saturday Night Fever," starring John Travolta, was a key factor in bringing disco music and culture to a global audience.

4. What is a common disco music feature?




Answer Info: Disco music often features the "four-on-the-floor" beat, a steady bass drum hitting on every beat in 4/4 time.

5. Which of these bands is associated with disco music?




Answer Info: The Bee Gees are iconic for their contributions to disco, particularly with hits like "Stayin' Alive" and "Night Fever."
