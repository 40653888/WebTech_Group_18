ü™© About Disco

Disco music emerged in the 1970s, known for its upbeat tempos, string sections, and funky basslines. It brought people together on the dance floor and celebrated freedom of expression, rhythm, and style.

Famous disco artists include Donna Summer, the Bee Gees, Gloria Gaynor, and Chic. Clubs like Studio 54 in New York City became legendary for their extravagant parties and iconic dance moves.
