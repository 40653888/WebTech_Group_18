About Disco

Disco is a genre of dance music that emerged in the 1970s, known for its steady four-on-the-floor beats, syncopated basslines, and lush arrangements. It was hugely popular in nightclubs and helped inspire the dance culture of today.

Popular Disco Dance Moves

  • The Hustle: A line dance made famous by Van McCoy’s song "Do the Hustle".
  • The Bus Stop: A choreographed group dance involving repeated steps and turns.
  • The Bump: A dance where partners bump hips in rhythm with the music.
  • The YMCA: Famous arm choreography from the Village People’s hit song.
