The Jazz Period

The Jazz period, as we know today is a upbeat and soothing form of music that conisists of a wide range of instruments such as the sax, drums, trumpet, piano, and a lot more including electrical intruments like a synthesizer or an electric bass. The range goes on. Jazz contains a lot of different unique characteristics which create a creative sounds, one of these sound staples are the excellent use of improvisations and solos, these exist as masterpieces to boost the unique induvidual talent and prowes of an artist and their respective instruments. In recent times, jazz has grown and grown and the need for innovation has become more and more dire yet more and more creative, the genre uses techniques like polyrythms where more than one rythmic pattern are played simultaniously. Polyrythms are a major staple of Jazz and require an insane amount of precision when playing. With the development of jazz music, there has been a surfacing new subgenres which build on what already exist.
